# Purpose

The Living Workspace is the heart of SmartFlow.

It is not a dashboard.

It is not a homepage.

It is the user's prepared digital workspace.

Every time the user arrives, Flow AI has already prepared the environment.

The workspace should answer one question:

> **What matters most right now?**

---

# Design Philosophy

Traditional dashboards present information.

The Living Workspace presents intention.

The interface should feel:

- prepared
- calm
- intelligent
- alive
- personal

The user should immediately feel:

> "Flow AI has already organized my day."

---

# User Journey

The experience begins immediately after the Launch Experience.

Sequence:

Void

↓

Orb thinks

↓

Orb makes a decision

↓

Workspace is created

↓

Greeting

↓

Today's Focus

↓

Timeline

↓

Context Cards

↓

Ambient Life

---

# First Impression

The first visible screen should never overwhelm the user.

Instead of showing many widgets, SmartFlow should present a clear starting point.

Priority order:

1. Greeting
2. Today's Focus
3. Timeline
4. Supporting Context

# Screen Layout

The workspace is divided into three zones.

---

## Zone 1 — Presence

Location:

Top Left

Purpose:

Permanent Flow AI presence.

Contains:

- Orb
- Context Rail
- Current module
- AI state

---

## Zone 2 — Intention

Largest area.

Contains:

Greeting

Today's Focus

Timeline

Recommendations

This is where the user begins.

---

## Zone 3 — Context

Secondary information.

Cards.

Status.

Documents.

Upcoming events.

Habits.

Everything supporting today's work.

---

# Flow AI Presence

Flow AI never disappears.

It always exists somewhere in the interface.

States:

Resting

↓

Thinking

↓

Listening

↓

Speaking

↓

Preparing

↓

Waiting

Every state has its own motion language.

---

# Orb Behaviour

The Orb communicates intelligence.

Movement should follow:

Pause

↓

Think

↓

Decide

↓

Move

↓

Rest

Never:

Instant movement

Instant appearance

Instant disappearance

---

# Today's Focus

This is the most important card.

It answers:

What is the most valuable thing I should do now?

Only one primary focus should exist.

Everything else is secondary.

---

# Timeline

The timeline is not a calendar.

It is today's story.

Morning

↓

Afternoon

↓

Evening

Flow AI decides what deserves attention.

---

# Context Cards

Cards should never compete.

Each card has a clear priority.

Cards may include:

Tasks

Projects

Calendar

Finance

Health

Learning

Documents

Family

Weather

Habits

Cards appear only when relevant.

---

# Motion Philosophy

Motion explains intention.

Ambient Motion

↓

Presence Motion

↓

Interaction Motion

↓

Transition Motion

↓

Creation Motion

Animation is never decorative.

---

# Ambient Life

Nothing is completely static.

Examples:

Orb breathing

Ambient light

Soft gradient drift

Tiny border pulse

Occasional shimmer

Everything remains calm.

---

# Color Philosophy

Dark-first.

Deep background.

Soft gradients.

Minimal accent colors.

Light represents Flow AI.

Darkness represents space.

---

# Typography

Large breathing space.

Minimal text.

One clear message at a time.

No visual overload.

---

# Empty States

Empty never means blank.

Flow AI always explains:

what happened

what to do next

how to begin

---

# Interaction Rules

Hover should feel alive.

Click should feel intentional.

Nothing should jump.

Everything should flow.

---

# What the Workspace Should Never Feel Like

Never:

Traditional dashboard

Admin panel

Business software

Widget collection

Analytics page

---

# Success Criteria

The user should feel:

"I didn't organize my day.

Flow AI already prepared everything."

If this feeling is not achieved,

the workspace is not finished.
