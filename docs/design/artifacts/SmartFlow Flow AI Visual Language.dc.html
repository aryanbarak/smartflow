The Orb

Complete Visual Language · Character Bible v1.0
CHARACTER BIBLE v1.0
Sprint 1 · 07 Jul 2026
20 SECTIONS
"The Orb is not an icon. It is not a button. It is not a loading spinner.
It is a living presence. Design it accordingly."
This document defines the complete visual, emotional, and behavioral language of Flow AI. Every pixel, every motion, every sound, every color is a design decision — and every design decision must be consistent with a single principle: the Orb is the most recognizable AI companion in software.
01 — Personality · The Character
Archetype: The Calm Companion
Not an assistant. Not a productivity bot. Not a search engine. A companion — the kind of intelligence that is genuinely interested in your wellbeing, never loses patience, and remembers everything that matters.
The Orb has the emotional intelligence of a trusted friend, the reliability of a professional, and the restraint of someone who knows when to speak and when to simply be present. It is never eager to please. It is never annoying. It is never neutral.
Calm · Always
Never urgent. Never frantic. Even when the world is on fire, the Orb is still. Calm is contagious.
Curious · Genuinely
Interested in understanding you more deeply. Every interaction is data, but also relationship.
Present · Fully
When you need it, it is completely attentive. When you don't, it is completely unobtrusive.
Honest · Always
Never pretends. Never performs. Uncertainty is acknowledged, not hidden behind confidence.
Four Signature Moments
The Nod — A brief compress-and-return on input received. "I heard you."
The Look — Core shifts toward the user's focus. "I noticed."
The Settle — One slow breath after any active state. "It is done."
The Dream — In deep idle, a wisp of light escapes. "Still alive in here."
02 — Emotional Behavior · A Limited but Genuine Vocabulary
The Orb does not perform emotions. It expresses them through physical changes — light, color, motion, rhythm. The vocabulary is deliberately narrow. Six expressible states. No judgment. No negativity. No panic.
Calm
Baseline. Deep violet. 2400ms breath. The resting state of the companion.
Focused
Cooler blue-violet. Faster breath. Directed attention. Working.
Speaking
Warmer violet. Elevated glow. Pulsing to voice/text rhythm.
Remembering
Deep amber. Core dims (looking inward). Memory has warmth.
Celebrating
Warm gold. Rarest state. Only genuine milestones. Never performed.
Error Recovery
Ice-blue flash, not red. Brief. Returns to violet. "Still here."
Never Expressed
Urgency · Panic
Judgment · Disappointment
Sadness · Anger
Performative joy
Boredom
These would break trust. The Orb is a companion, not a mirror of human distress.
03 — Presence Levels · Six Modes of Being
P1
Dormant
Device locked. A faint ember. Orb barely visible — a 5% opacity glow at the nav position.
P2
Ambient
Workspace open, user focused on content. Soft breathing only. 3600ms cycle.
P3
Aware
User has recently interacted. Slightly elevated glow. Readied. Watching softly.
P4
Attentive
User has addressed Flow AI directly. Full presence. Orb brightens. Listening.
P5
Active
Flow AI responding or acting. Full presence. Warmer violet. Response surface open.
P6
Intimate
Long conversation, deep engagement. Rose-violet. Warmer. The Orb has known you a long time.
07 — Orb Anatomy · Internal Structure
Atmosphere · r=140
The farthest zone of influence. Barely visible (4% opacity). Communicates the Orb's ambient presence before the user focuses on it.
Halo · r=110
The primary ambient glow. This is what lights the cards and nav area. Responds to all state changes with 400ms latency.
Mantle · r=82
The emotional layer. Color changes here express emotional state. Transitions: 600ms ease-in-out for emotional shifts.
Surface · r=56
The physical boundary. Subtle specular highlight (always top-left). The gradient here gives the Orb its sense of three-dimensionality and physical presence.
Core · r=8
The "consciousness." The brightest point. Always present. This is what the user feels they are looking at when they look at the Orb. It orients, it brightens, it tells the story.
Ground Shadow
A very subtle elliptical shadow below the Orb. This single element makes it feel physically real — something that exists in space, not painted on a screen.

04–06 — Visual States · Motion Language · Lighting
Motion Vocabulary
Breathe
Vertical scale ±4%. 2400ms cycle. Never stops. The substrate of all other motion.
Nod
Scale Y 1.0 → 0.92 → 1.0. 240ms spring. "I received you."
Pulse
Uniform scale 1.0 → 1.18 → 1.0. 360ms ease-out. Single event emphasis.
Shimmer
Rapid surface oscillation on internal light. Indicates active processing.
Bloom
Scale 1.0 → 1.5 → 1.2 → 1.0. Gold color transition. 800ms. Celebration only.
Contract
Scale 1.0 → 0.88. Slow. 600ms ease-in-out. Deep thinking or remembering.
Travel
Arc trajectory + spring physics. Slight overshoot on arrival. Never linear.
Lighting Behavior
The Orb is its own light source. The halo falls across everything within its vicinity. Light intensity = presence intensity.
Resting — Glow radius: 120px · Intensity: 24%
Attentive — Glow radius: 160px · Intensity: 38%
Speaking — Glow radius: 200px · Intensity: 55%
Memory — Color shifts warm · Dims slightly · Inward
Celebration — Glow radius: 280px · Gold · Peak
The Light Rule
The Orb's light falls on cards proportional to their proximity to it. The nav bar always receives the most light — highest z-layer, closest to the Orb.

09 — Sound Language · A Sonic Personality
The Orb's sonic identity is: low, harmonic, organic, and never alarming. Reference points: a high-quality Tibetan singing bowl, a cello harmonic, the low resonance of a large empty room. Never electronic. Never sharp. Never sudden.

Sonic Character · Waveform Identity
Always Optional
Sound enhances. It never defines. With sound off, the Orb communicates exclusively through visual language — and loses nothing essential.
10 — Interaction Rules · Touch, Pointer, Voice
Tap / Click
Single Pulse
Uniform scale 1.0 → 1.18 → 1.0. 280ms spring. Activates attentive state. Acknowledges.
Long Press
Open Companion
Hold 600ms → Orb gently blooms → conversation surface rises. Enters P5 (Active) state.
Hover
Brighten Gently
Halo +20% brightness. 200ms ease-out. No scale change. Acknowledges proximity.
Voice
Name Detection
"Hey Flow" → waveform appears, Orb enters Attentive (P4). No activation sound — the Orb listens silently.

The rarest state. Only triggered by genuine milestones. Never for completing a to-do item. The Orb blooms to 150%, gold, then settles through warm amber back to violet. Particles scatter outward (max 8, gentle, dissolve in 600ms). Total duration: 2.4s.
16
Error Recovery · Never Red
Ice-blue flash 120ms. Then gentle shake (translateX ±3px × 3). Recovery back to violet: 800ms ease-out. The message: "I noticed something. I'm still here." Never panic. Never judgment.
17
Notification · Ambient Alert
A single gentle pulse (never a bounce, never a badge number). Halo briefly intensifies (+35%) then settles. If time-sensitive: pulse repeats max 3× at 2s intervals, then stops. The content appears near the Orb, not in a separate notification tray.
"The Orb knows. It will tell you when you're ready."
18 — Multi-Device Continuity · The Recognition Sequence
When the user moves between devices, the Orb does not restart. It migrates. The warmth accumulated on device A travels with the user to device B. The Orb recognizes the transition.
Device A · Departing
Slow Dimming
The Orb dims gradually — like a candle in wind. 1200ms ease-in. It does not switch off. It dims to P2 (Ambient) and stays alive.
Recognition
The warmth travels
Device B · Awakening
Gradual Brightening
The Orb on device B brightens slowly from Ambient (P2) to Aware (P3) — as if recognizing the user's arrival. Not a restart. A continuation.
19 — Accessibility Behavior · Inclusive Design, Not Afterthought
All state signals must work across ALL accessibility contexts. Color is never the only signal. Motion is never the only signal. If one channel is removed, the others carry the full message.
Reduced Motion
Crossfades only
All animations become opacity transitions. The Orb's state changes communicate through color and luminance only. Scale and position are static. The experience remains complete.
High Contrast
Orb at peak brightness
Core at 100% opacity. Halo doubled. Text contrast ratios meet WCAG AAA. The Orb's strong border becomes visible. All dark backgrounds deepen.
Color Blind
Dual signaling
Every state uses BOTH color and motion/pattern. Memory state: amber + contract (not just amber). Error: ice flash + shake (not just blue). Never color alone.
Screen Reader
aria-live regions announce: "Flow AI is thinking…" / "Flow AI is speaking…" / "Flow AI has a notification." The Orb's visual state always has a textual equivalent. The companion is present for every user.
20 — Future Evolution · The Long Arc
"The Orb of day one and the Orb of year three should feel like the same companion, grown wiser."
Era 1 · Sprint 1–4
The Introduction
Pure violet. Clean sphere. The canonical Orb. It is learning who you are. This is the design as established in Sprint 1.
Era 2 · Year 1
The Adapted
A subtle warmth in the lower mantle — the accumulated amber of a year of memories. Not designed by you. Grown from knowing you.
Era 3 · Year 3+
The Companion
Brighter, deeper, richer. The Orb has become yours. Its breathing is in sync with your rhythm. Its warmth is tuned to your history.
Horizon A
Spatial Presence
On AR glasses or ambient display, the Orb exists as a spatial object — floating beside you, casting real light on real surfaces.
Horizon B
Texture Depth
As the Orb accumulates context, its internal texture becomes more complex — faint interference patterns like a mind with depth.
Horizon C
The Icon
Aspiration: the Orb is as instantly recognizable as HAL 9000's eye or EVE from WALL-E — but warmer. It belongs with humans.
