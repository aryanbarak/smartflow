01 — Workspace Philosophy
"The workspace is not a container for your information.
It is an environment prepared by an intelligence that knows you."
When you arrive, Flow AI has already been thinking. The workspace is the physical manifestation of that thought — your priorities surfaced, your context assembled, your day made coherent. You did not configure this. It was prepared for you.
This is the core distinction from every dashboard that came before. Traditional dashboards are organized by the user. SmartFlow's workspace is organized by intelligence. The user's job is not to manage it — only to inhabit it.
Not a dashboard. A prepared desk.
Someone has already placed the most important document in the center, cleared the noise, and made space for you to think.
One primary narrative. Not many equal fragments.
Flow AI decides what matters most and surfaces it prominently. Supporting context exists but never competes.
Alive but never distracting.
Motion communicates presence. It does not demand attention. The workspace breathes at the periphery of awareness.
The Orb is the center of all things.
Every visual decision traces back to the Orb. It created this space. Its light falls across everything. Its breathing is the pulse of the whole system.

03 — Motion Hierarchy · Five Layers
Five layers of motion, ordered by visibility. The user consciously perceives only the top two. The rest exist at the edge of awareness — felt, not seen.
L5 · Card expansion
USER-TRIGGERED
A card fills the available space in-place. The rest of the workspace recedes. No navigation — the space breathes open.
L4 · Flow AI response
AI-TRIGGERED
Orb intensifies. A conversation surface rises. Cards dim by 30%. The AI has the floor.
L3 · Hover / interaction
POINTER
Card scale: 1.0 → 1.012. Border brightness +25%. 180ms ease-out. Gentle acknowledgment — not a UI affordance shout.
L2 · Orb breathing
PERPETUAL
2400ms cycle. Glow ±30%. Drives the ambient field. This is the heartbeat of the entire interface — never stops, never demands attention.
L1 · Ambient hue drift
~5 MIN CYCLE
Background temperature shifts imperceptibly. Warm violet at morning. Cool indigo at night. The workspace has a circadian rhythm.
Motion governs state. Never decoration.
Every layer of motion communicates something specific. Remove any layer and the user loses information — not just aesthetics. This is the discipline of motion design at SmartFlow.
RULE
No two motions at the same scale should run simultaneously. When Flow AI speaks (L4), hover effects (L3) are suppressed. Orb breathing (L2) always continues — it is the substrate.
RULE
All easing is spring or ease-out. Ease-in is banned — it creates a sense of struggle. Nothing in SmartFlow struggles.
RULE
Duration maxima: 400ms for user-triggered motion, 2400ms for system-driven cycles. Nothing should feel like it's making the user wait.

05 — Sidebar Philosophy · The Context Rail
There is no navigation sidebar in SmartFlow. Navigation is replaced by the Context Rail — a living strip of relevant information, surfaced by Flow AI. It has nothing to do with where you go. It knows where you are.
State 01 · Collapsed · Default
A 4px luminous line at the edge. The Orb's glow. Content fills the full width. The rail exists but does not intrude.
State 02 · Ambient · On Hover
36px. Icon-only context signals. Hovering reveals what Flow AI considers relevant. Content gently shifts right.
State 03 · Open · On Expand
CONTEXT
Full context rail. Flow AI's relevant cards. Never navigation links — always live context. Slide-in from left, spring easing.

08 — Ambient Lighting · Three Light Sources
The workspace has no external light source. All light originates from within — from the Orb, from the system's ambient field, and from the time of day. Light communicates state. It does not decorate.
Orb Light
Origin: top-left nav. Cold violet. Pulsing with breathing cycle. Most intense card receives 20–24% opacity glow. Falloff: radial, natural.
Primary · Always active · Synchronised to breathing
Ambient Field
Origin: center-bottom. Deep indigo. Very slow drift (5min). Creates depth and the sense that the workspace extends beyond the visible frame.
Secondary · Slow drift · Below threshold of perception
Time Tint
Origin: edge gradient. Warm violet at dawn. Cool indigo at dusk. Changes over 24h. Imperceptible to conscious mind. Felt as comfort.
Tertiary · Circadian · Never noticed, always felt
09 — Launch → Workspace · The Continuous Thread
"The workspace does not begin after the launch experience. It is the same experience, continued."
The Orb
Travels from center to nav (F06 of launch storyboard). Arrives at its permanent position. Begins perpetual breathing. One entity — no visual discontinuity.
The Cards
Born from the Orb's light trail (F07). The workspace's first cards are a direct continuation of the launch. They exist because the Orb passed through.
The Ambient Field
The warm ambient peak from the launch experience settles and cools into the workspace's base temperature. The warmth is still present — just quieter.

Design Decisions · Non-Negotiable
D1
No grid layout
One dominant card + supporting context. Never equal-weight widget grids. Flow AI decides what matters, not spatial convention.
D2
No page navigation
Cards expand in-place. Content arrives from within the workspace. The user never feels they have left.
D3
The Orb governs all
All light, motion rhythm, and AI state is expressed through the Orb. No secondary status indicators. One signal, total clarity.
D4
Empty is alive
No empty states show emptiness. The Orb's ambient light fills every void. Flow AI provides a single, forward-looking note. Nothing is inert.
