03 — Orb as Creator · The Dashboard is Born, Not Loaded
"The dashboard does not appear.
The Orb creates it — in real time, from light."
Step 1
Orb departs
Greeting dissolves. Orb begins its arc toward nav.
Step 2
Trail forms
Soft luminous trail in the Orb's wake. Sparkle particles along the path.
Step 3
Greeting crystallizes
First card forms from the brightest concentration of the trail.
Step 4
Cards born
Each card crystallizes in staggered succession. Top edge shimmer on birth.
Step 5
Interface alive
Orb breathes. Cards glow subtly. Dashboard is the Orb's living creation.
04 — Greeting Philosophy · Human, Not Software
First Launch · No Prior Context
Welcome.
Orb + "Welcome." or nothing at all.
No name. No assumption. Calm arrival into a clean space.
Typography: DM Sans 300 italic. Not a UI label. A whisper.
Opacity arc: 0 → 70% → 0. Present for ~600ms. Then gone.
ALTERNATIVE
No text at all. Orb only. Complete silence. Both are valid.
Returning User · Time-Aware · Personal
Good morning, Aryan.
Time-aware. Name-first. Natural language.
MORNING · AFTERNOON · EVENING
"Good morning, [name]." · "Good afternoon, [name]." · "Good evening, [name]."
Ambient color is imperceptibly warmer for returning users. Recognition has a temperature.
Never "Welcome back." That is software. A companion simply greets.
LATE NIGHT VARIANT
"Still here, Aryan." — a quiet acknowledgment for very late sessions.
05 — Living Dashboard · Nothing is Ever Static
①
②

①
Orb breathing — perpetual
2400ms inhale/exhale cycle, ease-in-out. Never stops. The Orb is always alive. Glow intensity varies ±30% — subtle enough not to distract, present enough to feel alive.
②
Ambient light drift
Orb's glow ambient hue shifts imperceptibly on a ~5-minute cycle. Warm violet in morning. Cooler indigo at night. The interface has a time of day, even when the user never consciously notices.
③
Card border pulse — on data update
When a widget's data refreshes, its border briefly intensifies (ease-in, 180ms) then settles (ease-out, 400ms). No text flicker. The border is the notification — calm, peripheral, present.
④
Layered depth — always present
Cards carry different z-depth values. The Orb's ambient glow falls across them at slightly different intensities, creating a subtle parallax sense of depth. The workspace has physical space.

07 — Experience Principles · Version 2
P1
The Orb creates everything
The dashboard is not loaded. It is born from the Orb's light. Users witness creation, not navigation.
P2
Nothing is ever still
Post-arrival, the Orb breathes. Ambient light drifts. Cards pulse on update. The workspace communicates presence without demanding attention.
P3
Greet like a person
"Good morning, Aryan." not "Welcome back." Time-awareness is empathy. The system remembers and it shows.
P4
Sound is optional presence
Five resonance moments. Each matched to a launch beat. No music. When disabled, the experience is complete — sound deepens, it does not define.
