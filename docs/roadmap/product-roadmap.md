# SmartFlow Product Roadmap

**Document:** Product Roadmap  
**Version:** 1.0  
**Status:** Approved Product Strategy

---

# Vision

SmartFlow is evolving into an AI-powered Life Operating System.

Every release should move the product closer to one goal:

**Reduce cognitive load by allowing Flow AI to understand, organize, and guide the user's entire digital life.**
